# Neubrutalism Design System

Bold, high-contrast, structural anti-design graphic system inspired by Web 1.0, pop art, and brutalist architecture.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

**Brand Personality:**
- Raw, unfiltered, and honest
- High impact and attention-seeking
- Unapologetic, quirky, and asymmetric
- Swiss-influenced yet anti-establishment

**Key Characteristics:**
- Thick, stark black outlines on all components
- Hard-edged, solid black offset drop shadows (no blur)
- Flat, highly saturated pastel or neon background fills (zero gradients)
- Clashing color schemes and unexpected layout choices
- Prominent grid lines, borders, and structured frames

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

**Primary Colors:**
| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Stark Outline | Solid pure black | `#000000` | Borders, text, outlines, drop shadows |
| Solid Background | Pure white | `#FFFFFF` | Core workspace, card backgrounds |
| Muted Grid | Dark slate gray | `#1E293B` | Structural lines, table cells |

**Accent Colors (High Saturation Pastels):**
| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Cyber Yellow | Vibrant yellow | `#FFE600` | High-impact callouts, badges, hero buttons |
| Hot Magenta | Neon pink | `#FF2E93` | Alert badges, primary links, active tabs |
| Acid Lime | Electric green | `#A3FF12` | Success indicators, secondary callouts |
| Sky Cyan | Bright turquoise | `#00F0FF` | Primary button hover, highlight backgrounds |

---

## 3. Typography Rules

**Font Family:**
- Primary: Bold, high-character sans-serif (e.g., "Impact", "Arial Black", "Helvetica Bold")
- Monospace: For labels and metadata (e.g., "Courier New", "Consolas")

---

## 4. Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use thick `2px` or `3px` solid black borders on all elements
- Use pure solid black shadows offset directly to the bottom-right (e.g., `4px 4px 0px #000000`)
- Keep backgrounds 100% flat with vibrant pastel colors
- Keep layout components asymmetric and raw

### Don'ts
- Don't use soft blur shadows (e.g., `0px 4px 10px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`)
- Don't use smooth gradients or metallic finishes
- Don't use muted/desaturated corporate gray backgrounds
- Don't align elements with standard corporate grids
