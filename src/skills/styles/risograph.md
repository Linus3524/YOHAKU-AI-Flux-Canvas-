# Risograph Design System

Tactile, grainy print style mimicking traditional stencil-printing technology with vibrant fluorescent inks and imperfect overlay layering.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

**Brand Personality:**
- Artistic, indie, and hand-printed
- Tangible, textured, and warm
- Intentionally imperfect and human-made
- Retro-modern publishing aesthetic

**Key Characteristics:**
- Fine, organic grain and halftone dot textures (resembling physical ink on paper)
- Misregistration (slight alignment offsets between different colored ink layers)
- Translucent ink blending (where two inks overlap to create a third color)
- Limited color palette (usually 2-3 vibrant ink overlays)
- Warm, textured cream or off-white paper stock backgrounds

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

**Primary Colors:**
| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Paper Base | Warm cream stock | `#FAF6EF` | Background texture |
| Risograph Black | Warm charcoal | `#2A2A2A` | Typography, main outlines, dark shadows |

**Fluorescent Ink Colors (2-3 selected per print):**
| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Riso Pink | Fluorescent pink | `#FF4B93` | Highlight shapes, primary icons |
| Riso Blue | Medium indigo blue | `#2D68C4` | Primary drawings, secondary outlines |
| Riso Yellow | Bright dandelion | `#FFD23F` | Background accents, overlay mixes |

---

## 3. Typography Rules

- Keep layouts resembling posters, zines, or flyers.
- Use bold, high-contrast serif or clean geometric sans-serif to cut through the grainy texture.

---

## 4. Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use heavy, visible dot grain textures on all shaded areas
- Offset color layers slightly (e.g., green fill shifted 2px away from the black outline)
- Let overlapping translucent colors create secondary shades (e.g., blue + yellow = green overlap)
- Use warm paper textures for the canvas

### Don't
- Don't use smooth digital gradients or airbrushed lighting
- Don't align colors perfectly to their borders
- Don't use pure black backgrounds or glossy plastic surfaces
